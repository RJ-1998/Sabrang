ABOUT SABRANG

Sabrang is a Techno-Management-Cultural festival organized every year by JK LAKSHMIPAT UNIVERSITY Jaipur where students, artists and performers showcase their talent and skills. Here at Sabrang you become a part of a ride which will lead you to a destination where you discover the lights of happiness and colours of joy. Take a leap with us and feel the heartbeats dancing on invicible music. Starting from 26th October-28th October join us and get a bundle of unforgettable memories and experience at Sabrang!
